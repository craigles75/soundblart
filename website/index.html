SoundBlart

A clean, simple soundboard for your virtual meetings.

Download for macOS Download for Windows

Downloads are served via GitHub Releases. After publishing, upload SoundBlart-macOS.zip or SoundBlart-Windows.zip assets to the latest release.

Simple 3×3 Grid

Trigger sounds instantly with a tidy 3×3 layout. Scroll when you have more than nine sounds.

Folder‑Based Panels

Organize your sounds into folders on disk. SoundBlart discovers them at startup.

One Sound at a Time

Click to play. Click again to stop. Starting a new sound stops the current one.

Master Volume

Adjust a single volume slider that applies to all sounds.
